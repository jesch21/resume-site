Local Grocery

Groceries Galore!

Located on Court Street, Lyncourt Grocery Outlet is a well-known destination for residents looking for budget-friendly grocery items. The store offers a wide variety of groceries at discounted prices, from fresh produce to frozen foods. Shoppers can expect to find many off-brand products, which are often significantly cheaper than their name-brand counterparts, making it a great spot for families looking to save money on their weekly grocery bills.

Situated conveniently near local residential areas, Lyncourt Deli is more than just a corner store. It offers a selection of grocery staples, snacks, and quick meals, catering to those who need something fast and easy. This shop was my go-to throughout my childhood, as it was a quick walk from home. I remember saving up all my cash as a kid, even going so far as to collect stray coins in my house, to walk there after school for a bag of chips and a bottle of lemonade.

Just steps away from the Lyncourt Grocery Outlet, the Speedway gas station adds another layer of convenience for Lyncourt residents. Beyond just fuel, Speedway provides a selection of snacks, beverages, and everyday essentials, which is helpful for anyone in need of a quick grab-and-go option. As I mention more in the Hang-Out Spots page, Speedway is a great place to get some snacks and chill.

A short drive from Lyncourt, the Wegmans on James Street offers a more comprehensive shopping experience. Known for its wide selection of high-quality groceries, prepared meals, and specialty items, Wegmans is a go-to destination for those looking for fresh produce, gourmet foods, or specific dietary products. As I'm sure residents of Rochester are very familiar with Wegmans, they'll be happy to know that this Wegmans maintains it's quality and freshness. And the Wegmans sub is still up there as one of my favorite subs of all time.
