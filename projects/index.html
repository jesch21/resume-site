The Benefits of IPsec Tunneling

When: 2025

In my 6th semester at RIT, I took an Advanced Routing and Switching class. Our primary assignment was a group research project where we had to choose a routing and switching topic to research and test in a lab, collecting metrics to support our research. I worked with Ian Wu and Jamin Chen, and we chose to test the benefits of IPsec tunneling by simulating a tunnel in a lab environment and testing it's resilience against some simple security threats. The primary deliverable of this project was a final research paper.

The Corona Cinema

When: 2024

Since 2023, my friends and I continued a tradition of watching a movie together on a Discord call, a tradition first put together during quarantine to provide a new way to hang out that wasn't in person. At the time we managed the movie scheduled on a google sheet, but in 2024 I decided to create a website in my spare time for our movie nights. I titled it The Corona Cinema after the name of our covid-era discord call channel, and have since continued adding to and improving it's features and functionality. I even went so far as to purchase it's domain.
